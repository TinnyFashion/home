Our Story, Woven with Passion

Crafting Elegance for Every Woman

Tinny Fashion was born from a dream to make high-quality, beautiful garments accessible to women in semi-urban and rural areas. We believe that style is a way to express who you are without having to speak. Our collections for ladies and girls are designed with a deep understanding of local tastes, vibrant colors, and comfortable fabrics.

We are more than just a shop; we are a part of the community, celebrating the grace and strength of every woman.

Our Journey

Aug 2023

The dream begins. We secured the perfect spot for our shop and started building our vision from the ground up.

Sept 13, 2023

Grand Opening! Our doors opened for the first time, and we made our very first sale. A day we'll never forget!

Oct 15, 2023

Adding a little sparkle. We introduced a curated collection of cosmetic items to complement our garments.

Nov 7, 2023

Expanding our collection. We introduced a new line of tops and readymade items, offering more variety to our customers.

Jan 15, 2024

For the young fashionistas. We added a new range of girls' pants to our collection, catering to the younger generation.

Feb-Mar 2024

Refining our focus. We phased out some cosmetic items and gents' kids' garments to specialize in what we do best: ladies' and girls' fashion.

Jan 1, 2025

A fresh new look. We renovated and modified our shop to create an even better shopping experience for our growing family of happy customers.

Present

Still growing, still smiling. We continue to evolve, driven by the love and support of our happy customers.
